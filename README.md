# Documentation

## What problem does the project solve?

When a new student (freshman) enters UPB, they often waste time searching for basic and repetitive information such as:
- Where is a specific building or office located?
- How do I get to a classroom or university service?
- Where can I ask common questions about courses or administrative procedures?
- What are the tutoring or advisory schedules?
- What services does the university offer and how are they used?

**Our project** is a **web and mobile application extension** aimed at **new students at Universidad Pontificia Bolivariana (UPB)**, which centralizes practical support tools in a single platform:
- **University map** to help students locate buildings and key services.
- **Help forum** where students can ask and answer questions.
- **Advisory and tutoring schedules** for quick consultation.
- **Chatbot** that answers frequently asked questions from freshmen.

The main goal is to reduce confusion, misinformation, and time loss during the first weeks of university, improving the student onboarding experience.

---

## Target audience

**Primary users**
- **UPB freshmen (first-semester students)**.
- Newly admitted or prospective students during the induction process.

**Secondary users**
- Student mentors, tutors, or monitors who participate in the help forum.
- Academic or administrative staff (if official information is integrated into the platform).
